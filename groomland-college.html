Who We Are

A School Built on Excellence & Christian Values

Groomland College is a leading private school located at 368 Kidron Valley, Carrick Creagh Estate, Borrowdale, Harare. We offer both ZIMSEC and Cambridge curricula from ECD A through to Form 6 (A-Level).

Our school is built on Christian values, with qualified and experienced teachers, a small student-to-teacher ratio for personalised learning, and a safe, supportive environment for every child to thrive.
